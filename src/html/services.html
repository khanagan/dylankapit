Services Offered

Neurodivergent Relationship Coaching

Relationship Coaching is available to support neurodivergent individuals with their intimate relationships.

Dylan has personal and professional experience providing support to neurodivergent individuals around relationships.

Their dissertation focus is understanding how neurodivergent people experience sex and dating, and they are looking forward to supporting you with your relationship(s).

General Consulting

Dylan helps schools with creating inclusive classrooms for LGBTQ+ and neurodivergent students. They have done their Autistic Sex Ed presentation at local, state, and national conferences and for schools across the country.

Tutoring

Dylan can provide academic support for students of all ages. They have 17 years of tutoring experience, with much of that experience being for neurodivergent and disabled students.

While they can tutor in all subjects, their favorite subject to tutor is Math.
